# Loop Report

## Loop

Name and ticket or quest step.

## Product Outcome

What a player can now do or what decision can now be made.

## Evidence Boundary

What remains simulated, browser-dependent, subjective, or unproven by listening.

## Changes

Files, contracts, commands, and UI surfaces.

## Verification

Commands and browser checks actually run, with exact results.

## Musical Evidence

What was heard or interacted with, and what cannot be inferred from automation.

## Adversarial Review

Failures, misleading states, second-owner risks, and how they were handled.

## Documentation

Queue, current plan, orbit note, and durable contracts updated.

## Git

Branch, local commits, push/PR state, and untouched worktrees.

## Remaining Risks

Known limitations without optimistic wording.

## Next Activation Signal

The observable result that makes the next loop ready.
